University of Maine Men's Club Hockey Team - 2016

Welcome to the unofficial website of the University of Maine Men's Club Hockey Team. We are a group of developing young men who work hard and have fun both on and off the ice. We have a commitment to ourselves, our family, our community, and our university. Each day we look to represent these groups with the highest amount of chivalry.
